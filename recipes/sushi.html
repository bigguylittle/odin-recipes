Sushi

Description

Sushi is a Japanese dish, a very healthy snack usually consisting of raw fish, seaweed, rice, and other vegetables or additions. Fun Fact: Sushi is traditionally eaten by hand.

Ingredients

This is what you will need to make your sushi :)

  • Fish of your choice i.e salmon, squid, crab
  • White Rice
  • Seaweed
  • Cucumber
  • Soy Sauce
  • avacado

Steps

These are the steps you will follow to make your sushi

  1. Cut up your fish
  2. Roll the sticky rice out so it is flat
  3. Lay your seaweed flat
  4. Lay your flattened sticky rice on the seaweed, it should cover the surface
  5. Slice your cumber thin and add it on top of rice and seaweed
  6. Do the same to the avacado
  7. Add your sliced fish as well
  8. Roll it all up
  9. Slice the rolled seaweed into edible portions
